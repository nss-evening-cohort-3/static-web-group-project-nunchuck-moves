About Farmer Ted's Soups

Our story

When Theodore Humphrey came to the great state of Tennessee in the early 1900's, all he wanted to do was start a farm and provide a good home for his family of five. Theodore was able to do just that and became a legend before his time, by choosing to oversee his farm in the most natural way possible - never sucumbing to the scorge of chemical pesticides, carefully keeping bees to properly enhance his crops, using complicated crop-sharing techniques to make sure the dirt never wore out and even persuading all of his farm hands to use a composting toliet to provide the best fertilization possible.

Our mission

In keeping with the ways of Grandpa Theodore, our beloved Farmer Ted, we at Farmer Ted's Soups strive to provide you with the most delicous, healthy and heart-warming soup you will ever taste. We use and raise only wholesome, Oregon-certified organic, plant-based ingredients and you can rest assured that all of the animal products we use in our soups are farm-raised and have not been treated with antibiotics or hormones. We work diligently with our partner-farmers to make sure we all keep the highest standards to provide you and yours with a hearty yet wholesome bowl of pure goodness.
